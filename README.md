# Toast It! 🍞

**Toast It!** is a fun social couch co op multiplayer game where players work together to create different types of toasts. 


---

## About the Toast!

Toast It! will test your communication and gameplay skills, 1-4 players are situated around a table. Players work as a team passing different ingredients around the table to help each other make toast. Players work against the clock in multilple rounds to make as much toast as possibe and get the highest score!

---

## How It Toasts! 

### Setup and Lobby
* **Host & Join:** This game was desiged on xcode for IOS, and the iPhone 16/17. It may not properly run on other Apple devices 

### Setup and Lobby
* **Host & Join:** One player taps host to create a lobby and all other players click join and wait for the host to configure the game 
* **Seating Order:** The game uses a table for the host to check who joins, and to arragne the table based on irl for passing ingreidents to each other.

### Gameplay Mechanics
1. **The Golden Rule:** All Toasts require butter to start! 
2. **Dragging & Dropping:** Assemble your toast by dragging ingredients on the toast and plate in the middle of the screen. 
3. **Passing Ingredients:** Players get different ingredients, and collaborate by passing them off your screen and to your other team mates. 
4. **Submitting Orders:** Once your toast looks good, press submit and send off your toast! 
5. **Beat the Clock:** The resturant is a demanding place, make sure to send the correct toast in the right amount of time! 

### Game Structure
Toast it! progresses over 3 different rounds that will test your friendship and toast making abilities! 
* **Round 1 (45 seconds):** Make Plain Butter Toast and Egg Toasts!
* **Round 2 (30 seconds):** Add on Strawberries and Cream or Avocado Toast.
* **Round 3 (15 seconds):** The final toast! Make as many as possible

## 💻 The Toast Stack!

* **Language:** Swift
* **Front End:** UIKit, AutoLayout, and custom gestures like UIPanGestureRecognizer and UISwipeGestureRecognizer
* **Networking:** Apple `MultipeerConnectivity` framework for local matchmaking, lobbies, and game sync (note that the lobby system is currently hardcoded for easy game connection)
* **Architecture:** Model View Controller 
* **Audio:** Custom controller for background music and sound effects
